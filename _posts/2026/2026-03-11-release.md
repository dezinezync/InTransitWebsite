Building on top of Pockity's [October 2025 release](https://pockity.app/blog/2025-10-15/release/) came the January 2026 release and now the March 2026 release.

Improvements and fixes from both v2026.1.0 and v2026.3.0 are outlined below:
---
### Improvements
- Redesigned the "What's New" screen to be more beautiful and informative 
- Optimized receipt scanning with Apple Intelligence for faster results and lower battery consumption 
- Improved iCloud sync reliability during first setup, ensuring a smoother experience when restoring your data 
- Refactored the entry editor for a snappier feel and improved overall stability 
- Enhanced the reliability of recurring expenses and notifications to prevent duplicates across multiple devices 
- Fixed an issue where the app could appear stuck while syncing data from iCloud 
- Significant performance and stability improvements to the app's database for a faster, more responsive experience

### Fixes  
- Fixed entry editors displaying the amount incorrectly or only partially
- Fixed a long standing bug causing Widgets to incorrect datasources and cause crashes
- Fixed a long standing bug causing the editor to incorrectly persist data to disk
- Fixed a long standing bug causing the timeline to not reselect the selected entry when tapping on the edit button to open the editor
